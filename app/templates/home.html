Front-End Design

Our front end services begin with a clean, responsive design. We use HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and Font-Awesome to create the overall look and feel of your site. We design mobile-first, to ensure a great looking site across all devices. This ensures your users will be able to access, interact with, and navigate your site with ease.

UI/UX

Our UI/UX, or User Interface/User Experience is designed to be fluid, seamless, and interactive. Browsing your site should be easy and fun. We accomplish this through Bootstrap, JavaScript, and jQuery, to make everything on your site, from filling out a contact form, to learning about a new technique, intuitive and entertaining.

Back-End

The back-end of your site is where all the "stuff" happens, from validation of forms to routing between pages. We prefer to use Python 3 and Flask as a back-end package, simply for it's lightweight, easy to expand package. With Flask, the back-end controller is extremely fast, improving your overall user experience, as your site will load faster, no matter the device.

Databasing

Ah, the database. A place to store and maintain large amounts of information. If you have a large menu, selection of services, or lots of customers, then a database is the place to store that info. We like to use SQLite for small to medium databases, and MySQL for the big ones. We then take this information and can display it in many ways, anywhere on your site.
